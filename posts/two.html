Q

Identify the purpose, program, and users of the building

A

The Jawahar Kala Kendra was built as an "international art and culture center" for Rajasthani heritage. It caters to indigenous folk singers, dancers, art-connoisseurs / scholars, artists / artisans, and tourists. It also treated as a public museum that Rajashtan government oversees.
